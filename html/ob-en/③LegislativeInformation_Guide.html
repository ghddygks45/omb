The Office of Foreign Investment Ombudsman has prepared the regulatory information service regarding legislations on foreign investment to help foreign investors solve grievances that occurred in their business activities.

What is the Regulatory Information Service for Foreign Investors?

The Regulatory Information Service provides information on legislations related to local regulations which may affect foreign investors and allow them to submit their opinions on such legislations.

Regulatory Information Service for Foreign Investors
  • Government Legislation
    • New and stricter Acts related to regulations(enforcement decree, enforcement rule, public notice) proposed by the government will be summarized and translated in English.
    • The original amendments in Korean is also provided. Submissions made within the deadline will be delivered to the competent ministry.
  • National Assembly Legislation
    • New and stricter Acts related to regulations proposed by the National Assembly will be summarized and translated in English.
    • The original amendments in Korean is also provided.
      Submissions made within the deadline will be delivered to the competent ministry.
