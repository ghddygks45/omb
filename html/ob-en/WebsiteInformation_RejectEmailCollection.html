  • Reject Email Collection
The website of the Office of the Foreign Investment Ombudsman is strictly banning unauthorized collection of email address from this website, in accordance with the concerned law below. Usage of unauthorized email collecting programs or other technical devices to collect email address will be given serious penalties accordingly.
Act on Promotion of Information and Communications Network Utilization and Information Protection, etc., Article 50-2 (Prohibition on Unauthorized Collection of Electronic Mail Addresses)
  • 01
    No one may collect electronic mail addresses from an internet homepage without prior consent of the operator or manager of the internet homepage automatically by a program for collecting electronic mail addresses or any other technical device.
  • 02
    No one may sell or distribute electronic mail addresses collected in violation of paragraph ①
  • 03
    No one may knowingly use an electronic mail address, the collection, sale, and distribution of which are prohibited pursuant to paragraphs ① and ②, in transmitting information.
